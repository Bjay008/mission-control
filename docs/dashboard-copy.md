# Dashboard copy

## Product descriptor

Mission control keeps long-running missions moving. by coordinating AI, tools and people, with clear, explainable decisions.

## Demonstration mission

- Mission: Bible in 365 days
- Status: Awaiting human approval
- Current stage: Quality Control complete
- Owner: You
- Reason for pause: This decision belongs to a human.
- Next step: Review the Quality Control result and approve continuation.

## Timeline captions

- Mission created — Goal, owner, and next step recorded.
- Research complete — Sources gathered and handed to Planning.
- Plan approved — The 365-day structure is ready for execution.
- Draft generated — AI completed the assigned work.
- Quality Control complete — Output passed automated checks.
- Human decision required — The mission paused intentionally because this decision belongs to a human.
- Approval recorded — Mission Control will preserve the decision and its reason.
- Execution resumes — The mission keeps moving with a clear next step.

## Reusable interface lines

- Every task has an owner.
- Every handoff has a reason.
- Every mission has a clear next step.
- Not a failure. A human decision.
- Decision recorded.
- Mission resumed.
- Every mission deserves mission control.
